I — The Temple

Step inside, at your own pace.

Myohokkein has stood on the slopes of Kobe for nearly four centuries — a branch temple of the Nichiren school, open to visitors, pilgrims, and the simply curious.

II — Visit

How to find us.

Myohokkein sits fifteen minutes' walk northwest of Shin-Kobe Station, tucked into a quiet residential street at the foot of the Rokko range. All are welcome during open hours.
